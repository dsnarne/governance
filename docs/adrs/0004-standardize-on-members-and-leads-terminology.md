# 4. standardize on members and leads terminology

Date: 2026-04-27

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Formerly, we used the term "contributors" and "maintainers" to refer to people
working in a team. This works well in the Tech committee since they are existing
projects that need to be maintained and accepts outside contributions. However,
in Labrador, each team is working from scratch and the teams are contained within
the Labrador organization, so the terms "member" and "lead" are more appropriate.
This also alignes with the terminology used in the past two years of the Labrador.

## Decision

We standardize on the terms "member" and "lead".

## Consequences

All existing code and documentation will be updated to use the terms "member" and
"lead" instead of "contributor" and "maintainer".
